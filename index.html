Operation Details

Recover Only: Performs a mass erase of the device using the CTRL-AP (Access Port #2). This will erase all flash memory and unlock the device if it was protected. No firmware will be written.

Factory Reset: Performs a mass erase followed by writing the factory firmware (firmware.hex) to the device. This restores the device to its original factory state.

OpenBlink XIAO nRF54L15 Firmware Flash: Performs a mass erase followed by writing the OpenBlink firmware (openblink_XIAO_nRF54L15.hex) to the device. This firmware is from the openblink_XIAO_nRF54L15_Sense repository.
